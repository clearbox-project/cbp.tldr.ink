Budgeting with Mental Buckets

Misbehaving Section II. Mental Accounting. Chapter 9 Buckets and Budgets

Mental budgeting (with specific money buckets) makes money not purely fungible as Econs predicts.

  • With budgeting buckets set up, people are less likely to move money "freely" between diffferent buckets. Thus, money becomes not fundgible as many economists assumed in their theory or models.
