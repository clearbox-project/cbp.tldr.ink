In Economics, sunk cost should not be considered in opportunity cost when make new decisions.

  • human tend to use sunk cost as an "anchor" or take it as part of opportunity cost when making new separate decisions.

e.g. If tickets to a concert are very expensive, we are more likely to go despite of harsh weather condition. The price we paid for tickets should be considered as sunk cost.
