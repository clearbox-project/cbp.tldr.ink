A reason to do things

Influence Chapter 1 Weapons of Influence p. 4.

When we ask someone for a favor, we will have higher chance of success by giving them a reason (might not be logically sound).
People simply like to have a reason for what they do.

E.g. May I borrow your pen because I'm in a rush? Thank you!
vs.
May I borrow your pen?
