Prospect Theory - Under- or over-weighting probabilities

People will unconsciously consider and act on an outcome with a probability of 99% as if its probability was 95%; and an outcome with probability of 1% as if it had a probability of 5%.

  • weighting, here, is not the same as estimating - different from Overconfidence Effect.
