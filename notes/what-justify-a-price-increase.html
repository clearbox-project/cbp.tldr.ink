What justify a price increase

Misbehaving Chapter 14 What Seems Fair

Customers often can justify price increase due to unsustainable profit margin/increase in production cost.

However, the Endowment Effect makes it harder to increase base price - but easier to go back to “suggested detail price”.

Inflation itself can give fake increase or decrease effect in prices.

  • thanks to inflation, your absolute salary will "increase" very year.

Price increase due to sudden increase in demand might lead to long term loyalty loss under certain circumstances, sellers need to be wise and human about it.

  • 在自然灾害时期哄抬必要物资

At the core, don’t over charge for the service provided, otherwise, customers can’t justify their purchase and will not come back.

  • Chicago's Alinea's chef only charge customer the amount that he feels reasonable and worth the value, even though their demand is very high.
